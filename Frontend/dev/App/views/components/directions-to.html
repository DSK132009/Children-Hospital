DRIVING DIRECTIONS TO UMC HOSPITAL

From the South via 95
  • Take US 95 North to Rancho Drive
  • South on Rancho Drive to Charleston Blvd.
  • East on Charleston Blvd.
  • UMC is on the North (left) side of the road
From the South via I-15
  • Take I-15 North to West Charleston
  • UMC is on the North (right) side of the Road
From the North via 95
  • Take US-95 North to Rancho Drive
  • South on Rancho Drive to Charleston Blvd.
  • East on Charleston Blvd.
  • UMC is on the North (left) side of the road
From the North via I-15
  • Take I-15 North to West Charleston
  • UMC is on the North (right) side of the Road
