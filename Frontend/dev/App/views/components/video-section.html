UMC TRANSPLANT PROGRAM PATIENT VIDEOS

Nevada’s first kidney transplant took place at UMC, Nevada’s only transplant center, on Christmas Day 1989. Since then many patients have received the gift of life through the transplant program. The UMC multidisciplinary transplant team is comprised of physicians, nurses, pharmacists, dietitians, social workers, financial counselors and Living Donor coordinators who will happily assist you with all your transplantation questions and concerns.
